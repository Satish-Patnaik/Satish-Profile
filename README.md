# Satish Patnaik Baggam, PhD  
Molecular Biologist ‚Üí Bioinformatics & Computational Biology  
Assistant Project Scientist | UC Irvine


I am a molecular biologist with 10+ years of wet-lab experience in ocular and retinal disease research, currently transitioning into bioinformatics and computational biology.

My work sits at the intersection of experimental biology and omics data analysis. I specialize in gene expression and RNA sequencing of eye tissues, with a focus on the lens, retina, and ocular surface. I bring deep biological context to data analysis, not just statistical outputs.

**Research focus**
- Retinal vascular diseases (Retinopathy of Prematurity)
- Age-related Meibomian gland dysfunction
- Lens development and aging

**Computational skills**
I actively analyze large-scale omics datasets and am building production-level analysis workflows.

Languages
**R** (primary for omics analysis)
**Python** (data handling, visualization, Scanpy workflows)

**RNA-seq and bulk transcriptomics**
- DESeq2
- edgeR
- Differential expression analysis
- PCA
- GSEA
- Quality control and experimental design

**What this GitHub contains**
This repository documents my transition into computational biology through:
- Reproducible RNA-seq workflows
- Single-cell and single-nuclei analysis pipelines
- Exploratory data analysis notebooks
- Visualization and interpretation focused on biological insight
